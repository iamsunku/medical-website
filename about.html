Our Story

Founded with a mission to deliver quality medicines, MEKSTONE PHARMACEUTICALS LLP combines scientific rigor with manufacturing excellence. Our focus is on accessible health solutions across multiple therapeutic segments including general medicine, pediatrics, and nutraceuticals.

We partner with WHO–GMP certified manufacturers and perform strict QA at every step — from raw material testing to finished product release.
